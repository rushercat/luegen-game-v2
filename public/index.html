How to play
  • The first player picks a Target Rank (any rank except Jack).
  • On your turn, play 1–3 cards face down claiming they match the rank.
  • Jacks can never be the target rank — you can only play them as bluffs.
  • Only the next player may call LIAR!
  • If they were lying → liar takes the pile, challenger starts the next round.
  • If they told the truth → challenger takes the pile, is skipped, the player after them starts.
  • Empty your hand → you've won and sit out the rest of the game.
  • With 3+ players, the game ends as soon as only 2 are left holding cards — both lose. With 2 players, the last one with cards loses.
  • Hold all 4 Jacks → instant loss for you, everyone else wins.
  • Hold all 4 of any other rank → discard them (revealed for 15s).

Game Modifiers

Lean Deck
Drop cards before dealing — more pressure to bluff. Jacks and jokers are never dropped, and removals are spread evenly across the remaining ranks (round-robin), so you never lose two of the same rank while another stays full.
0
Loaded Pile
Pile starts with N face-down cards — first wrong LIAR pays heavily.

Trickle Mode
Max cards per play (default 3). Lower = longer, more cautious games.
3
Rotating Target
After every N plays, the target rank flips to a random other rank. Cards already on the pile become safe from LIAR. Jacks are never picked, and discarded ranks are off-limits. 0 = off.
0
Jokers
Wild cards added to the deck — they always count as the target rank. In Liar's Bar mode, jokers also auto-scale up so every player gets exactly 5 cards.
0
Wild Suit
All cards of the chosen suit count as the target rank when revealed (always truthful) — like extra jokers spread across every rank.
